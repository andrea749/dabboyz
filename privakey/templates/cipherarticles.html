Caesar Cipher

The Caesar cipher is a classic cipher. The Caesar cipher, also known as a rotation cipher, essentially involves shifting the alphabet by a certain number. So in a Caesar cipher of 1, 'a' becomes 'b', 'b becomes c, and so on with 'z' becoming 'a'. So in a Caesar cipher of 1, 'dadboyz' becomes 'ebecpza'
